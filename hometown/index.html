About Trenggalek

Trenggalek is a beautiful regency located in East Java, Indonesia. Known for its serene beaches, lush pine forests, and iconic caves, it offers a peaceful atmosphere away from the city hustle. The community is warm and welcoming, and the culinary scene is rich with local delights such as Nasi Lodho and Sego Gegog.
