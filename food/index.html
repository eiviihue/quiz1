Nasi Lodho
A traditional dish made of smoked chicken cooked with coconut milk and spices, served with rice and vegetables. The smoky flavor makes it unique to Trenggalek.

Alen-Alen
A crunchy ring-shaped snack made from tapioca flour and spices, often enjoyed as a light bite or gift to bring home from Trenggalek.

Sego Gegog
A rice dish wrapped in banana leaves and steamed with spicy anchovy sambal. The fragrance from the leaves adds a special taste.
